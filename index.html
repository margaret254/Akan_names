AKAN_NAMES:

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Below are two columns that show the akan_names given to both females and males according to the weekday they were born.

Male Akan_Names.

  • Monday : Kwadwo
  • Tuesday : Kwabena
  • Wednesday : Kwaku
  • Thursday : Yaw
  • Friday : Kofi
  • Saturday : Kwame
