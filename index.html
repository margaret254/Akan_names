AKAN_NAMES:

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.

Below are two columns that show the akan_names given to both females and males according to the weekday they were born.

Male Akan_Names.

  • Sunday : Kwasi
  • Monday : Kwadwo
  • Tuesday : Kwabena
  • Wednesday : Kwaku
  • Thursday : Yaw
  • Friday : Kofi
  • Saturday : Kwame

Female Akan_Names.

  • Sunday : Akosua
  • Monday : Adwoa
  • Tuesday : Abenaa
  • Wednesday : Akua
  • Thursday : Yaa
  • Friday : Afua
  • Saturday : Ama
